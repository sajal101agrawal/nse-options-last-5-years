Verification Summary

The verification process checked the IV and RV calculations for validity and consistency. Most checks passed, with only the IV Term Structure Check showing anomalies.

The IV Term Structure Check failure indicates that in 379 out of 1234 instances (30.7%), the IV for shorter expiry options was higher than for longer expiry options. While this is not the typical pattern (longer expiry usually has higher IV), it can occur during periods of market stress or when there are specific events expected in the near term.

All RV checks passed, indicating that the Realized Volatility calculations are valid and consistent.
